Networking Lunch

The Rocky Mountain Medical Technologies Conference is pleased to provide a networking lunch for students, faculty, biomedical industry professionals, and medical professionals.

The lunch will take place from 12:00 - 1:00 PM on Monday, February 25, 2013, in the University Memorial Center on CU's campus.

  • If you are a CU student please register through this link. You will be re-directed to the CU-Boulder Career Services website. Please log-in or create an account in order to register for the conference as a CU student.
  • For non-CU students, the networking lunch is included in the registration price.
