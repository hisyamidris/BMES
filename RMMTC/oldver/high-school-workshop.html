High School Workshop

For questions, please click here to contact the Workshop Coordinator.

The Rocky Mountain Medical Technologies Conference is pleased to announce a medical technology workshop geared specifically for high school juniors and seniors. This workshop will provide an opportunity for our younger participants to gain hands-on experience with a medical technology and learn more about the broad range of sub-disciplines within biomedical engineering. Students will work on teams of four to build a model of a medical device.

The workshop will be held on Monday, February 25, 2013 from 1:15 - 2:30 PM at the University Memorial Center on CU's campus. Participation is limited to the first 50 high school individuals who register.
